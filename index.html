The real issue

Most businesses lose sales before the conversation starts.

A prospect can find you, read your website, even like what they see — and still not reach out. Not because your price is wrong. Not because they chose a competitor. Because something on your brand or website created enough uncertainty to make inaction feel safer than action.

That's decision friction. And most businesses don't know they have it.

  • Discovery calls that turn into explanation sessions
  • Enquiries that say "I'll think about it" and disappear
  • Prospects comparing you on price instead of value
  • A website that looks good but quietly isn't working
